About Pet Paws Journey

We are dedicated to making pet transport safe, transparent, and stress-free for every pet owner.

Our Story

Born From a Love of Animals

Pet Paws Journey was founded in 2019 by a team of animal lovers and logistics professionals who saw a critical gap in the pet transport industry. Too many pets were being shipped without adequate tracking, safety protocols, or transparency.

We set out to build a platform that treats every pet like family. Our technology-first approach combines real-time GPS tracking, climate-controlled vehicles, and professional handlers to deliver a premium transport experience.

Today, we have safely transported over 15,000 pets across all 50 states, earning the trust of families, breeders, and rescue organizations nationwide.

What Drives Us

Our Mission & Values

Every decision we make is guided by our commitment to animal welfare and customer satisfaction.

Safety First

Every vehicle is climate-controlled and GPS-monitored. Our handlers undergo rigorous training and background checks.

Full Transparency

Live tracking, instant notifications, and detailed status updates keep you informed at every stage of the journey.

Compassionate Care

We treat every pet as our own. Regular feeding, exercise breaks, and affection are standard on every journey.
